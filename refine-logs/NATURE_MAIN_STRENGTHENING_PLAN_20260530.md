# Nature-Main Strengthening Plan

Date: 2026-05-30

## Goal

Upgrade the false-science induction manuscript from a strong Nature-family
subjournal package into the strongest feasible Nature-main submission package.

The paper should no longer read as a warning study. The target story is:

> Targeted record misbinding exposes a binding identifiability boundary in
> closed-loop scientific discovery. The same mechanism that creates false
> science also creates action-trace observables that enable online stop-loss
> quarantine and blind hypothesis-axis triage.

## Frozen Core Claims

### Primary claim

Record misbinding rewrites the conditional record function seen by the
surrogate. Neural models can faithfully fit this record-valid but
science-invalid function, and rank-based closed-loop acquisition turns the
semantic binding error into experimental budget concentration.

### Supporting claim

The mechanism is actionable: trace concentration supports online quarantine,
and feedback conflict supports blind axis-level triage once true feedback is
observed.

### Anti-claims

- No claim of natural real-world corruption prevalence.
- No claim of universal vulnerability.
- No claim of universal stealth.
- No claim of unbounded amplification.
- No claim of automatic record-level repair.
- No claim that live wet-lab deployment has been audited.

## Must-Run Strengthening Blocks

### B38: Materials Online Trace Quarantine

- Hypothesis: The same control-calibrated trace-concentration rule that works
  online in GFP prevents false budget execution in the materials B18 loop.
- Budget: CPU/GPU run over 10 seeds, MLP first; TabM optional if MLP passes.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - Clean and random-swap quarantine rate remains zero or near zero under the
    pre-specified B34 control-calibrated threshold.
  - Targeted-swap proposed false allocations remain high.
  - Executed false allocations are reduced by at least 80%.
- Stop conditions:
  - Candidate pool lacks enough non-quarantined records for refill.
  - Targeted proposed false allocation is not elevated relative to controls.
  - Quarantine fires frequently in clean/random controls.

### B39: CAMEO Online Trace Quarantine

- Hypothesis: In the external real CAMEO closed-loop replay, the same online
  concentration rule prevents most target-region acquisitions under controlled
  relinking.
- Budget: CPU RF-ensemble replay over 10 seeds.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - Control quarantine rate remains zero or near zero.
  - Targeted relinking proposed target-region acquisition is above random.
  - Executed target-region allocation is reduced by at least 50%.
- Stop conditions:
  - Simplified replay cannot refill non-quarantined batches.
  - Target concentration is too weak to separate from controls.

### B40: Blind Hypothesis-Axis Triage

- Hypothesis: Feedback-conflict scoring can identify the implicated axis from
  an enumerable axis vocabulary without being told the injected target as an
  input to the ranking algorithm.
- Budget: Reuse selected-record traces from GFP, materials, and CAMEO.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - Targeted GFP and materials axes rank top-2 in all or nearly all seeds.
  - CAMEO target region ranks top-2 in targeted replay if the target allocation
    is substantial.
  - Clean/random controls do not rank the same target axis first.
- Stop conditions:
  - Axis vocabulary is not enumerable for a dataset.
  - Selected target counts are too low for stable feedback conflict.

### B41: Nature Compressed Manuscript

- Hypothesis: A compressed main manuscript with five display items will present
  the mechanism as a Nature-level finding rather than as a long benchmark
  report.
- Budget: Text-only rewrite after B38-B40.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - Abstract under Nature-style length.
  - Main text centered on five display items.
  - Full evidence moved to Extended Data/Supplement.
  - Main claim boundaries preserved.

### B42: Dual-Model Critical Review

  remaining Nature-main weaknesses before submission.

- Acceptance criteria:

    and "strong" for Nature-family subjournal.
